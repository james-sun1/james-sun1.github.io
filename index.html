Alpha Metallurgical Resources Stock Pitch

November 3, 2025

I pitched Alpha Metallurgical Resources alongside my good friends Joe O'Grady and Watson Meyer. This pitch is predicated on a stronger-than-predicted demand for met coal from Asia, particularly India, as an input for steel production. As one of the largest producers of met coal in the United States with zero debt on its balance sheet, AMR stands fundamentally sound in an era of reindustrialization, positioning it as an excellent investment opportunity.
